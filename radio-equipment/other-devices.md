# Other Devices {#other_devices}

Besides the standard UEs some other equipment is also available for specific tasks.
## SIM8200-m2
- HAT for raspberry pi
- 3G/4G/5G
- Snapdragon X55 chipset
- SA
- DL/UL: 120/40 Mbps (tested)
